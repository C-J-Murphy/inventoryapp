The Inventory App

The inventory app was created with small independent restaurants in mind in order to serve professionals in concentrating fully in their task by eliminating the burden of manual inventory tracking.

...

Features

Easily manage your inventory...

We can help you start by linking your inventory to your own database. After that, our product allows you to easily add and remove products and sections of your inventory at will. Automate your entire inventory process from processing inventory as it comes in to products being used. You can choose to automatically order more of a particular item when it reaches a certain point.


Pen and paper are a thing of the past...

Our product allows for the easy tracking of products by providing a navigational database that updates all of your current products. Our intuitive interface makes the process simple, just click the add button when an item enters your inventory and click the subtract button as you remove items from your inventory.

Pricing

The Inventory App is priced at $100 per license. With a purchase, we show you how to navigate the application and assist you in important your inventory into our database. Our application is designed to allow workers to be autonomous, they should be easily able to manage the database and inventory without further assistance.
